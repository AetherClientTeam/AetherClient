# Aether Gradient Team Colors

## Behavior

- `ae_gradient_team_colors` replaces flat DDTeam scoreboard backgrounds with a static gradient.
- The gradient fades from the original DDTeam color into black.
- No animation, timers, textures, or background workers are used.

## Acceptance

- Scoreboard team rows keep the original DDTeam hue.
- The gradient is clearly visible and does not spill outside the team group rectangle.
